Poem: "The Undermountain of Jainism"

This idealum quid explains an ironical scene in the Vaibhavadimpi.
There's a king who is being honoured by the Bra|hmins and who raises
his hand and sings a song which ends with the words: Ujareroi sab,
"May you attain liberation". Brahmins who are thus put on| their guard,
are charged with a great sacrifice of indescribable mass. Sword-bearing
warriors who prostrate themselves in bouts of ecstasy: "|The author has committed
a senseless act of cruel violence. The exceedingly miserable survival of that old
woman was a gross end."
